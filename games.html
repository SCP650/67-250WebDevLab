Games I'm Playing Right Now

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

What happens when you put Spiderman's movement mechanics, Dark Souls' gameplay and Assassin's Creed's stealthy together? And set the difficulty to expert mode? You get Sekerio.

Through trials and errors, players move through well-designed levels. I really enjoy the combat and the juiciness in this game but I think the default controller config can be better designed.

This is a good game, but God of War is better. God of War allows beginners to enjoy the story while allowing hardcore gammers to focuing on the combats. This game only allows the latter.

Platform: PS4

Rainbow Six Siege

The best online tactical shooter game I have ever played. The focus on tactical defense and insertion, environmental destruction and cooperation between teammates give the player a highly immersive experience.

Platform: PC

A Plague Tale: Innocence

This game tells a tale of two orphans in the time of the Black Death. Touching, thrilling and unforgettable.

Platform: PC

Ring Fit Adventure

This game is probably going to stay in this section for a long time. I'm spending half an hour on this game every day. It's a great game that successfully merges exercising in the real world with gameplay in the virtual world.

Platform: Switch

Games I've Played in 2020

Super Mario Odyssey

A simple game that is surprisingly fun to play. I played the game with friends at parties, played it single on airplanes. I never thought collecting coins can be so much fun.

Platform: Switch

Uncharted 4

The first few chapters are certainly fun to play. My heart rate will spike as I climb through the buildings. But as the game progress, the gameplay became repetitive.

Platform: PS4

Supraland

A fun and casual puzzle solving game! If you enjoyed the puzzles in Portal and the adventures in the Legend of Zelda, you should definitely try this game out!

Platform: PC

Ni No Kuni
